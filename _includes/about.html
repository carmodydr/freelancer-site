About

Hi, I'm Dan Carmody-Morse. I am a data scientist, physicist, teacher, and writer living in Durham, North Carolina.

When not behind a computer or in front of a class, I can often be found running or biking around on nearby trails.

My interests and pursuits are often rooted in the narrative of science and exploration of the natural world, especially exploring the intricate relationships between ecology and technology. I aim to re-establish a profound connection to nature, starting from the ground up, by seeking fresh perspectives on the natural beauty found in my immediate surroundings.
